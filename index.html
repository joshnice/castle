Who we are

Here at Castle Damp and Timber, as market leading damp specialists operating around Derbyshire, Nottinghamshire, North Leicestershire and all of Staffordshire, we pride ourselves in providing outstanding work with superb and friendly customer service.

Our principles

Customer Satisfaction

We endeavour to ensure that each and every customer gets the work that they both deserve require from us.

Excellent Results

We aim to ensure that cleanliness and quality are by words of our performance in every job that we complete. All our works come with long guarantees and are completed to our own high standards as well as that of the trading authorities of the UK.

Competitive Pricing

Our experienced and professional surveyors understand the need for trustworthy and fair pricing of our works, this is why for many years our company has made sure that we provide the best work for the best price that the customer can find.

Reliability

At Castle Damp and Timber, we recognise the importance of reputation and reliability for, as we believe, a company that you cannot trust is not a company that you would let into your home. For many years now, this company has ensured that not only do we make sure the customer is getting a good deal but also that our results are reliable.

damp & timber

All of our works are guaranteed and installed to British standards, in addition to this, we also offer insurance backed guarantees. Almost all of our work is both human and pet friendly meaning that there is no need to move out during the installation period. We are vetted and monitored by the UK Trades Confederation which ensures our continuing reputable and professional conduct, feel free to check us through an independent body or read our customer reviews. Our team has over 30 years of experience and are ready to help with any of your damp issues today, why not give us a call?

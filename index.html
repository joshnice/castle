Who we are

Here at Castle Damp and Timber, as market leading damp specialists operating around Derbyshire, Nottinghamshire, North Leicestershire and all of Staffordshire, we pride ourselves in providing outstanding work with superb and friendly customer service.

Our principles

Customer Satisfaction

We endeavour to ensure that each and every customer gets the work that they both deserve require from us.

Excellent Results

We aim to ensure that cleanliness and quality are by words of our performance in every job that we complete. All our works come with long guarantees and are completed to our own high standards as well as that of the trading authorities of the UK.

Competitive Pricing

Our experienced and professional surveyors understand the need for trustworthy and fair pricing of our works, this is why for many years our company has made sure that we provide the best work for the best price that the customer can find.

Reliability

At Castle Damp and Timber, we recognise the importance of reputation and reliability for, as we believe, a company that you cannot trust is not a company that you would let into your home. For many years now, this company has ensured that not only do we make sure the customer is getting a good deal but also that our results are reliable.

Survey

We offer a free no obligation survey to all property owners. We have a small marketing team who will arrange these surveys in particular areas for a limited time. This way we can utilise the time of our surveyors. There is NO charge for these surveys as we use our advertising budget to cover the cost. We find that people tend to come back to use our services in the future after learning how professional, honest and friendly we are. We gain a lot of recommendations from these surveys and, in our opinion, word of mouth is the best form of advertising you can have.

These surveys are for all property owners with a house over the age of 30 years. They are not just for people with visual signs of issues. Over 70% of the surveys that are completed are dry and given the all clear which give the house owner peace of mind. If any issues are picked up during the survey, the surveyors will explain the cause and advise of the most appropriate course of action to eliminate the issues.

The surveys usually take around 30 minutes if no issues are detected. If any problems are found it will take a little longer to correctly diagnose and advise. These surveys are non destructive and are done on the internal walls of the property. A visual check of the exterior will be carried out to check for masonry damage, leaking guttering etc.

The results of these surveys will be left verbally with the house owners and not passed onto any third parties. No personal information will be passed onto or sold to third parties.

If you have arranged a survey through one of our marketing team you will receive a phone call prior to your appointment time to confirm we are running to the allotted timetable. Please be advised that these surveys are very popular and sometimes re-arrangements may need to be made. We will also inform you of the name of the surveyor who will be calling. All our surveyors and marketing staff carry photo identification. If in any doubt please call 0800 345 70 83.
